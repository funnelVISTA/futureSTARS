Register for a Program

Sign your child up for any of our seven programs — sports, education, mentorship, and more. A parent or guardian completes this form. It takes about five minutes.

1

Parent or guardian

The adult responsible for the participant — this is who we'll contact.

2

Participant details

Programs are open to ages 3–19. Registering more than one child? Add them all here — you only fill in your own details once.

3

Emergency contact

Someone we can reach if we can't reach you.

4

Consent

A team member will confirm your child's place and let you know about any program fee. Questions? Get in touch.
